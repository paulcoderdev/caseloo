How secure is Caselo?

Caselo uses enterprise-grade encryption, PCI-compliant infrastructure and fraud detection systems to keep every transaction secure at all times .

Which payment methods do you support?

We support Cards, Bank Transfers, Moneygram, Cashapp, Mobile Money, Apple Pay, Google Pay, QR Payments, POS and USSD.

How long does settlement take?

Most settlements happen instantly, while some bank transfers can take up to 24 hours depending on the bank.

Can I integrate Caselo with my website?

Yes. We provide REST APIs, SDKs and plugins for easy integration with your website or mobile application.

Is customer support available?

Absolutely. Our support team is available 24/7 via Email, Live Chat and Phone.
